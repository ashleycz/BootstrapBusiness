Online Reviews

"Not For Counting Calories!!”
My rating:

Excellent diner style food, generous helpings and delicious fish and chips!! Old style diner takes you back in time when sock hops and 45's were popular. The waitstaff was polite, friendly and efficient. Serving sizes were very generous, two can share a platter easily. Great prices, great diner atmosphere.

sylvarstarwolfe2015 - TripAdvisor September 2016
“Always a good ... meal.”
My rating:

The food and friendly small-town atmosphere are great. The prices are very affordable. Dinner, including dessert and a coke, all for $13.00. A lot of landmark old diners decline but try to survive on their long time in business and name. Phillips doesn't do that, they make sure the food is still good. Small space, but always clean comfortable.

James M - TripAdvisor October 2016
